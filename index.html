Explore Cross-Platform Compatibility

Virtual reality headsets are compatible with a variety of platforms

It's worth noting that not all virtual reality headsets are compatible with all platforms. It's important to check the specific compatibility of a headset before purchasing it.

Test Compatibility

Personal Computer

Many virtual reality headsets are designed to work with PCs. These include headsets like the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and Valve Index.

PlayStation

Sony has developed its own virtual reality headset called the PlayStation VR. It is designed to work with PlayStation 4 and 5 consoles.

Mobile

There are also virtual reality headsets that are designed to work with smartphones. These include headsets like the Samsung Gear VR, Google Cardboard, and Google Daydream.

Standalone

Some virtual reality headsets are standalone devices that do not require a separate computer or console. Examples include the Oculus Quest and the Pico Neo.
